THE THESIS · JUL 6 · FOR THE JUDGES, FOR US

SHEET Nº 015 — THE CASE FOR ROOOT · THE GAP, THE BET, THE KEEP

Betting built the sharpest instrument sports has ever had: a live, de-vigged, second-by-second read of belief. It pointed that instrument at transactions. Fandom never got an instrument at all — it got a broadcast, a group chat, and a dozen tabs. ROOOT points the same honest signal at the thing betting never served: the room. No wager, no token, on our site — fans stay free to wager anywhere else, just sharper for having watched here.
