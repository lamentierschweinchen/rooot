# The crowd/market plate — generation prompt (owner pick: bespoke generate)

You generate the **plate** (the printed panel); I typeset the **live numbers** into the two
blank fields in-page, so the reading stays honest and updates with the match. That's why the
prompt insists the number areas render as clean flat fields with NO digits.

**Footprint:** wide horizontal panel, aspect **~2.6 : 1** (e.g. 1600 × 615), so it drops into
the terrace where the two-cell box sits now. Deliver a **PNG**, cream ground (not transparent).

---

## THE PROMPT (copy-paste)

> A wide horizontal printed data plate from a vintage soccer match programme, letterpress on
> warm cream newsprint. Two panels side by side, divided by one thin printed rule. The left
> panel is headed "THE CROWD SAYS" in small bold condensed caps; the right panel is headed
> "THE MARKET" in small bold caps inked in restrained antique gold. Under each heading sits a
> clean, empty, flat cream field with NO numbers and NO text — reserved space. Flat spot-ink
> printing only: no gradients, no glow, no drop shadows, no 3D. Bauhaus geometric restraint
> with Lichtenstein flatness. Fine benday halftone dot texture, subtle registration crop-marks
> in the corners, a hairline woven-thread selvage running along the bottom edge as a quiet nod
> to cloth. Palette strictly: warm newsprint cream #EDE6D6, press-black #1A1815, one team red
> #C8202A, one team blue #27427F, antique gold #B08D2F. Intimate Panini-sticker / ticket-stub
> collectible print quality. Editorial, deliberate, understated.

**Avoid (negative prompt):** gradients, neon, glowing edges, 3D render, photoreal, any people
or faces or crowds, stadium or pitch imagery, any logo or crest, FIFA marks, betting slip,
casino, odds board, UI buttons, sliders, drop shadows, watermark, lorem/placeholder numbers.

## Notes for a clean drop-in
- Keep the **two number fields flat and roughly centered** in each half — I position the live
  Doto/Anybody numerals over them, so the emptier and more even those fields, the better.
- Team red/blue are placeholders for the tint of the two heading rules — the live team inks
  ride in the typeset numbers, so the plate itself can stay mostly ink+gold on cream if the
  colored version feels busy. Generate one of each and we'll pick on the surface.
- If you want the **woven** alternative instead of printed paper: swap "letterpress on cream
  newsprint" → "woven into a fabric care-label patch, visible warp/weft threads, stitched
  heading, selvage edges" and keep every other line. Same footprint, same blank fields.
- Bring 2–3 generations; I'll composite the live numbers and screenshot it in the terrace so
  you judge it as pixels, on the surface — never as a loose image.
