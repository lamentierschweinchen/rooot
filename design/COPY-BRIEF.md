# ROOOT — Copy brief (per-surface, for owner review)

**Purpose.** A copywriter's worklist. Each surface below is a review unit: the offending
strings (with file:line), why they fail, the direction, and a **proposed line** to react to.
Review surface by surface — approve, tweak, or reject each proposed line; nothing ships unseen.

**Who this is for.** A copywriter (or me on your go) executes one surface at a time; you sign
off each before it commits. This brief is the instruction set, not the final copy.

---

## 0 · The voice (applies to every surface)

ROOOT reads like **a match programme, a ticket stub, and a terrace chant** — printed, plain,
warm. Not an app, not a dashboard, not a trading desk.

**Say things as they are** (owner's law: plain words first). Two truths that must never blur:
- **The market is a number.** A de-vigged probability. Show it plainly — a percent, or "favoured."
  Never name the plumbing.
- **The crowd is a roar.** Real counts of real people. **Never a percentage, never a mean, never
  blended with the market number.** "6 of 9 called England" — never "67%."

**Banned words/forms** (these are the betting-app/dashboard tell the brand dies on):
`DE-VIGGED` · `1X2` · `X` for a draw · `XP` · `devnet` / `mint` / `on-chain` as fan-facing jargon ·
decimal-mean scorelines set in score type (e.g. "NOR 1.3–1.3 ENG") · `TEST SEAT` · any glyph a fan
must decode (`◈`). If a number needs a legend, it isn't copy yet.

**One open decision that gates the showcase (yours):** the original thesis was "market belief as a
golden **tide** on a night pitch." The built product is cream paper + woven cloth + printed crowd
blocks — no night pitch, no literal tide. **Is "the tide" still the market metaphor, or retired?**
Every market-facing line below hangs on this. The proposals assume *retired, replaced by plain
"the market / the number."* Say the word if the tide stays.

## 1 · Showcase — the judge's front door  ·  `showcase.html:103,144`  ·  **BLOCKER**

**Wrong:** "Live market belief runs as a golden tide on a night pitch" (:103), "the golden tide"
(:144). Sells a product that wasn't built; a primed judge sees the mismatch on the first screen.

**Direction:** state what ROOOT *is*, in the print/cloth voice. Root once. The market's read shows
plainly. The crowd's roar is real and counted. At the whistle you keep what you lived.

**Proposed thesis:**
> **ROOOT is a match programme that comes alive on your phone.**
> You root once. The market's read prints beside the crowd's roar — real people, really counted,
> never faked. When the whistle blows, you keep what you lived: a woven scarf, a struck pin, a
> sealed poster of the match, yours for good.

## 2 · Gate — the turnstile  ·  `gate.html` (~:272 logic, market bar)

**Wrong:** "DE-VIGGED" on the market bar; the draw labeled **"X"**. Jargon on the second screen.
(Also a *logic* fix, tracked separately: a pure lurker can't enter without setting a score.)

**Direction:** market bar names a plain chance; the draw is "Draw."

**Proposed:** replace the "DE-VIGGED" caption with **"the market's read"** (or drop the caption and
let the three numbers stand under `FRANCE · DRAW · SPAIN`). Label the middle outcome **"Draw"**, never "X".

---

## 3 · Ground — the hub  ·  `ground.html`

**Wrong:** dial hint "LOOM · THE TIDE" (dead night-look language); footer "ANONYMOUS TEST SEAT"
leaks scaffolding; unexplained `◈` cabinet glyph; "XP 0 · 0–0" token.

**Direction:** dial hints name the room in plain words; footer names the seat honestly; no XP;
no undecoded glyphs.

**Proposed:** dial hint → **"LOOM · your match, woven"** (or just "LOOM"); footer "ANONYMOUS TEST
SEAT" → **"YOUR SEAT"** (or the real seat label once claimed); drop "XP 0"; replace `◈` with the
word **"Cabinet"**.

---

## 4 · Terrace — the crown jewel  ·  `terrace.html:256` + score row

**Wrong:** "XP 0" (:256); a decimal-mean crowd scoreline ("NOR 1.3–1.3 ENG") set near the real
score — a mean dressed as a scoreline is exactly the banned form; "TAP ANYWHERE TO CHEER" never
retires (stays up after you've cheered).

**Direction:** kill XP. The crowd's prediction is a *count* statement, never a mean-scoreline.
The cheer prompt retires once you've cheered.

**Proposed:** remove "XP 0". Replace the decimal scoreline with a count line, e.g. **"most fans
call it France by one — 14 of 22 seeing a France win"** (real counts). After first cheer, the
prompt fades to **"you're cheering"** (or just retires).

---

## 5 · Stadium — market stat-card  ·  `stadium.html` (market card subtitle)

**Wrong:** "THE 1X2 · DE-VIGGED" subtitle. The bet-type name + the vig math, on a card.

**Direction:** the card is "the market's read"; show the plain chance per outcome.

**Proposed:** subtitle **"THE MARKET'S READ"**; body shows `FRANCE 54% · DRAW 27% · SPAIN 19%`
(plain percents, no "1X2", no "de-vigged"). Empty pre-KO state → **"prints as the match plays."**

---

## 6 · Cabinet — the keepsake case  ·  `cabinet.html`

**Wrong:** "LOCKED · 1+" cryptic notation on ghost pins.

**Direction:** say what unlocks it, plainly.

**Proposed:** **"earn it at a match"** (or "locked — win it at a match"). Drop the "1+".

---

## 7 · Collect flow — the on-chain keepsake  ·  `terrace.html` (in progress)

Already being reworked to a single **"Collect"** button (was "KEEP IT FOREVER · ON DEVNET"), with
plain states — **Collect → Collecting… → Collected ✓ · view it ↗** and, on failure, **Try again ·
"your scarf is safe in your cabinet."** Listed here so you review the state micro-copy alongside the
rest when it lands. No "devnet", no "mint", no "forever" anywhere.
