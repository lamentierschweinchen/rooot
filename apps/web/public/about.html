ABOUT ROOOT

a match program that comes alive · version alpha

WHAT THIS IS

ROOOT is a free, live fan experience for the tournament. You take a side, predict the score, and watch the match weave itself — the market's read beside the crowd's roar. At full time you keep what you lived: the woven cloth of that night, yours.

FAN DATA, COMPUTED LIVE

Every number here is computed as the match runs: the live odds feed, the taps of the people in the ground, and the match's own record. Crowd counts are counts. The odds are the market's de-vigged read, shown plainly beside them.

Nothing here is a wager. The odds are a reading, and reading is free — there is no stake, no token to buy, nothing to flip.

YOURS, FOREVER

When you collect a scarf it's kept on your device instantly. If you choose to save it to an account, it's recorded to a key only you hold — made with your device's own Face or Touch ID, nothing to install and nothing to remember — and it stays yours on any device. Each scarf's record is public and permanent.

WHO MADE IT

Built by one fan and a small team of machines for the TxODDS hackathon, with live data from TxODDS. Questions, problems, ideas: github.com/lamentierschweinchen/rooot.
