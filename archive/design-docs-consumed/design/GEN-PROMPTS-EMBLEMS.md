## CONTROL — the pitch-body / territory-heat plate  *(stat-plate register, self-contained)*
> Design a flat vintage stat-plate, portrait format, in solid charcoal (#1A1815) plus one single
> flat accent colour, on a plain white or transparent background for clean keying — no gradient,
> shadow, texture, 3D, lettering, players, ball or arrows; pure geometry. Subject: CONTROL, "the
> pitch body." An abstract top-down football pitch reduced to a plain upright rectangle with a
> single halfway line, its interior paved with a coarse grid of flat square tiles whose size and
> weight swell toward the centre and one goal-end — a geometric heat-field of where the play lives
> and who owns the ground. It must read as territory and pressure, not a tactics diagram, in the
> register of a printed vintage stat plate.
