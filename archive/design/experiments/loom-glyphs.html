ONE ALPHABET

THE MARKS · PRINTED ON THE PLATE  ·  WOVEN IN THE LOOM
Same glyph, two materials. On the collectible plates it prints crisp; in the loom the identical mark is cross-stitched to the cloth's thread-grid — so the ground and the cards read as one language, not two. Each glyph stays bold and grid-friendly on purpose: that's what lets it survive being woven at ~13 stitches wide.
