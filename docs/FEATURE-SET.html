01

THE PREDICTIONS — THE BELIEF HARVEST

Pre-match: entry is an opinion. The gate takes a side, a scoreline, and the conviction dial ("how sure?" — non-financial by law, it scales what a right call earns). Locks at kickoff.
In-game: the bigger idea — calls placed during play, each stamped server-side with the market's live read at the second of the call, resolved by the next real event. One pre-match snapshot becomes a moving BET / BELIEVE / HAPPEN trajectory — the thing no book harvests (they read the public only from slips, after the stake). NEXT GOAL is the live v1: the example, not the feature.

THE EMOTIONS — HOW THE NIGHT FELT

Six emblems — joy · muscle · heartbreak · relief · anger · hope — always one tap away from the dock. Drama windows open on real triggers (goals, reds, VAR, near-misses, big market swings, the whistle); each window's crowd split is revealed, then kept. The symbols speak for themselves.

THE DATAper-moment emotion histograms · the night's roar curve (~30s samples) — the feel layer of the record

03

THE ROAR & THE PRESENCE — BEING THERE, COUNTED

Rooted counts per end — real fans, never padded, never a percentage. Cheering is rate-honest (a tap is a tap, volume is decayed loudness). Watch minutes and arrival waves land in the record.

THE POINTS — INTERACTING EARNS

One formula on every surface: the stamp, every cheer, every reaction, every minute watched — and at the whistle the exact-score or right-result bonus, scaled by how sure you said you were. In-play arrivals earn everything except the full-time bonus.

THE KEEPSAKES — WHAT A NIGHT LEAVES A FAN

At the whistle the woven cloth seals — collecting it is one tap, local, instant; saving it to an account (Face ID passkey, no seed phrase) mints a real on-chain scarf whose image is the fan's own woven night. Match cards carry three-state verdicts; pins rank fan virtues off the real record — earned, not bought.
