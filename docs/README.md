## The record

- [night-reports/](night-reports/) — one per real match, every number traced to a
  field in that night's capture. Five matches, Jul 9 → the final on Jul 19.
- [incidents/](incidents/) — what broke, why, and the rule that came out of it.
  The [premature seal of the final](incidents/INCIDENT-jul19-premature-seal.md) is

- [notes/](notes/) — working research: anchoring, crowd systems, diagnoses.
- [sprint-log/](sprint-log/) — the hackathon's operational scaffolding: runbooks,
  handoffs, match-night cues. Dated, kept, not maintained.
- [pitch/](pitch/) — the manifesto and the evidence behind it.
- [txline/](txline/) — the upstream feed's own spec and probes.
